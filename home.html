Information about PACE

What is PACE?

PACE stands for "Program for Adult Continuing Education," which aims to enhance and develop educational and professional skills in various fields.

What are the goals of PACE?

1-Improve the quality of education. 2-Enhance practical and theoretical skills. 3-Support learners in accessing better job opportunities.

What subjects or courses does PACE offer?

PACE offers a variety of courses in multiple areas such as technology, management, languages, and social sciences.

What are the benefits of participating in PACE?

1- Acquiring new skills. 2- Improving job prospects. 3- Expanding professional networks.
